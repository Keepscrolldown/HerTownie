Cookies Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit HERTOWNIE. They help us recognize your browser, remember your shopping bag items, and understand how you interact with our fashion store.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your shopping experience. This includes keeping you logged in, remembering your preferences, and showing you personalized style recommendations.

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Necessary for the website to function (e.g., Checkout, Login). Session
Marketing Used to show you relevant ads and fashion trends on social media. 30 Days
Analytics Helps us understand which collections are most popular. 2 Years

Managing Your Preferences

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent you from placing an order on our site.

Third-Party Cookies

We may also use cookies from trusted third parties such as Google Analytics and Facebook to help us analyze site traffic and run targeted marketing campaigns.
