Shipping & Delivery

Where Women Connect Locally

Processing Times

Every "Her Townie" piece is handled with care. Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days. Delivery times vary by location but usually arrive within 3–7 business days.

Cancellation Policy

To ensure our community receives their orders as quickly as possible, our system begins processing immediately.

Please note: Once an order has been shipped and a tracking number has been generated, we are unable to fulfill cancellation requests.

Tracking & Support

A shipping confirmation email with a tracking link will be sent to you as soon as your order leaves our boutique. If you have any questions regarding your delivery, our support team is here to help.
